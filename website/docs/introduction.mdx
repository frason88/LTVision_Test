### Introduction to LTVision
pLTV OST is an open-sourced library for generating customer insights, evaluating pLTV opportunity sizing and providing a starting point to explore pLTV models suitable to business needs.

### Our vision
Our Vision is to build awareness, education and a community of practitioners of pLTV, innovating and contributing to open source code
